Corporate Couture: The High Heeled Hustle

Game Pack

Duration: Approximately 20min

Players: 2-5 players

Reward: The end goal is getting the job.

Premise of game: Young interns enter the workplace for the first time, encounter problems, challenges, opportunities, benefits and have to navigate the circumstances they are put in. The game can be adapted to suit any of the end goals (celebrity stylist's assistant, etcâ€¦)

Objective

The objective of Corporate Couture is to be the first player to reach the "Job Offer" space at the end of the board, symbolizing successful job attainment, based on a degree of luck, but also using your knowledge to take advantage of opportunity card boosters.

Setup

  • Each player draws a character figurine, each with pre-set personalities and life situations that may dictate how their wheel moves count.
  • Set up the game board, which is laid out with spaces leading from "Start" to "Job Offer."
  • Shuffle and place the Activity Cards and Opportunity Cards in designated piles on the board.

Game Play

  • Players take turns spinning the wheel to move forward on the board. The number may multiply depending on who the character is.
  • If a player lands on an Activity Space, they draw an Activity Card which could either advance or set them back based on realistic work-related scenarios; these cards can affect other players' game status as well.
  • Landing on an Opportunity Space allows players to answer the question written on it, this question is read by another player. Answering correctly lets the player move up two additional spaces.

Activity Cards

These cards have random events that could be, "Receive a glowing performance review, move forward 3 spaces," or "Your project fails, move back 2 spaces." These are drawn by the players themselves and they can also affect the position of other players.

Opportunity Cards

Contain questions related to work scenarios and professional etiquette. Correct answers propel the player forward two spaces, while incorrect or no answers result in staying in place.

Winning the Game

  • To win, a player must have at least 1 "Opportunity" card.
  • Players must land exactly on the finish line to win. If a player is near the finish line but cannot land exactly on it on their round, they must continue around the board.
  • A player can also win if they land on an "Activity" card space that moves them directly onto the finish line, even if the move is not exact.

Moral of the Game

The real-life objective of this game is to demonstrate the imbalance of the corporate world in different settings. Do we all truly have the same chances? Are we treated the same when entering a work field? Different privileges can give you different benefits. This game is supposed to show different challenges that you can face in the real world and is supposed to spark dialogue to create potential changes within the industry we wish to work for.

How the Game Works

  • Starting the Game:
    • Players pick out figurines to navigate the board from a dustbag, and get assigned their random characters.
    • The "Nepotism baby" player starts each round. If "Nepotism Baby" is not drawn, all players spin a wheel, and the highest scorer goes first, with the order proceeding clockwise.
  • Movement:
    • Players move by spinning the wheels.
    • If a player gets the "Nepotism baby" figurine, their movement is always doubled, throughout the game.
  • Gameplay Interactions:
    • Players may land on spaces that allow them to pick up "Opportunity" or "Activity" cards.
    • Actions from different players can affect others' positions on the board, mirroring real-world industry dynamics.
